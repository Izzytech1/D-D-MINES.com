About D&D Mining

D&D Mining Co. provides Dungeon Masters and players with comprehensive resources for adding mining mechanics, mineral lore, and underground adventures to their campaigns.

Our community has been developing mining systems for tabletop RPGs since the early days of D&D, creating balanced mechanics that enhance gameplay without overwhelming it.
